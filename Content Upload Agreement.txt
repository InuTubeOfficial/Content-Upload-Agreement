Content Upload Agreement

This Content Upload Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between InuTube ("Company") and Content Uploader ("Uploader") for the purpose of preventing the uploading of illegal content onto the Company's platform.

Content Upload
Uploader agrees not to upload any content onto the Company's platform that is illegal, infringes on any intellectual property rights, or is prohibited by any applicable laws or regulations.

Prohibited Content
Uploader understands that the following types of content are prohibited from being uploaded onto the Company's platform:

Content that promotes or incites violence, hate speech, discrimination, harassment, or any form of illegal activity
Content that contains malware, viruses, or any other harmful software
Content that infringes on any intellectual property rights, including but not limited to, copyright, trademark, or patent
Content that is pornographic or sexually explicit
Content that violates any applicable laws or regulations
Liability
Uploader agrees that they will be solely responsible for any and all content that they upload onto the Company's platform. The Company will not be liable for any damages or legal consequences resulting from the uploading of illegal content by the Uploader.

Indemnification
Uploader agrees to indemnify and hold the Company harmless from any and all claims, damages, and expenses (including attorney's fees) arising from the uploading of illegal content onto the Company's platform.

Termination
The Company reserves the right to terminate this Agreement and delete any illegal content uploaded by the Uploader without prior notice.

Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of United States.

Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements between the parties, whether written or oral.

Amendments
This Agreement may only be amended in writing and signed by both parties.

Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue to be valid and enforceable.
